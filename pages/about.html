About Me

Storytelling is the process through which we share with another what it is to be a human being.

I am an Australian-born film / commercial director based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I moved here after living in Los Angeles for seven years where I studied and made theater and films, wrote and edited screenplays, and auditioned as an actor. I have a fundamental belief that all people are doing the best they can, and that means that through good storytelling, there’s real hope for a better world. I like to work with groups of ambitious, creative people, and I like to create environments where people deliver their best work. I am driven by several core values which guide my decision-making.

 

If we share these values, I want to work with you.

My Values

Community

We work together out of a commitment to the wellbeing of others.

Creativity

We generate, and communicate a clear point of view.

Excellence

We strive to create the best work we can, always pushing to the boundary of our creative constraints.

Growth

We learn and improve with every project.
