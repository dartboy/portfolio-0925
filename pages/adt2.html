Feel Safer at Home

This project began as an experiment where I wanted to merge horror with contemporary dance. What we created was stranger and more abstract than I first imagined, and we ultimately shaped the piece into an avant-garde ad for home security systems, which I think complement’s the piece’s darkly humorous edge.
